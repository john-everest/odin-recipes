OK so this might look very complicated but actually it is very easy to make spaghetti. It's mostly simple ingredients and a lot of love!

The requirements for making spaghetti:

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozarella
  • Some leaves of basil
  • Love and patience

Cooking instruction

Now off to actually cooking the thing:

  1. Get your kitchen ready
  2. Boil the spaghetti
  3. Wash the vegetables
  4. Cook veggies in a pan
  5. Mix spaghetti and veggies + sauce together
  6. EAT!
