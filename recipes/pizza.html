OK so this might look very complicated but actually it is very easy to make pizza. It's mostly simple ingredients and a lot of love!

The requirements for making pizza:

  • Dough
  • Tomatoes
  • Mozarella
  • Some leaves of basil
  • Love and patience

Cooking instruction

Now off to actually cooking the thing:

  1. Get your kitchen ready
  2. Make the dough
  3. Roll it out, in a circle that is
  4. Add tomato sauce and toppings
  5. Put in oven and keep an eye out
  6. EAT!
